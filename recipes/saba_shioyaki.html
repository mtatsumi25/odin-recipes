Saba Shioyaki

Description

This recipe creates a salted mackerel. Beware the strong fishy stench. This is best cooked outdoors, or in a a well ventilated area.

Ingredients

  • Filets of Mackerel
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Salt the mackerel liberally on both sides
  2. Cook mackerel filet 7min on each side using a grill (a hot cast iron would work as well)
  3. Enjoy!
