Gyudon

Description

This recipe combines thinly sliced beef with sake, shoyu, mirin, sugar to create a delicious, somewhat sweet beef that pairs well with rice, pickled ginger, and shichimi.

Ingredients

  • Thinly Sliced Beef
  • Onion
  • Shoyu
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Slice beef and onions
  2. Combine shoyu, sake, mirin, and sugar
  3. Simmer onions in sauce mixture for minutes
  4. Add beef to simmering mixture & simmer until thoroughly cooked
  5. Enjoy!
